Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our oil and gas consulting services, software tools, and project support.

What type of oil and gas projects do you support?

We support upstream oil and gas projects including asset evaluation, production review, field development planning, PSC economic analysis, and technical due diligence.

Can you support PSC economic evaluation?

Yes. We provide PSC economic analysis including cashflow modeling, NPV, IRR, payout calculation, cost recovery, gross split, sensitivity, and scenario evaluation.

Do you provide technical due diligence services?

Yes. We provide independent technical and commercial review to support acquisition, divestment, financing, project screening, and investment decisions.

Yes. Selected oil and gas tools are provided as free resources to support technical and economic evaluation activities.
