Our Consulting Capabilities

Reservoir & Production Engineering

Technical evaluation of reservoir performance, production behavior, reserves potential, and field optimization opportunities.

  • Production forecasting

Economic & Fiscal Modeling

Economic evaluation for upstream projects using cashflow modeling, fiscal terms, investment indicators, and sensitivity scenarios.

  • Development scheduling
